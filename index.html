Space Debris- A Blockade To Future Space Missions?

Abstract

Half a century into the space age, we have expanded the limits of exploration, but we have carried our habit of spreading filth even into this domain as well. Space debris, which stands today as unnoticed as a black cat in a coal mine is culminating to hault the progress of space technologies.

We are putting so much debris in space that we may close ourselves from off from space due to the dangers of the space travel, posed by our own garbage heap! -Neil DeGras Tyson
